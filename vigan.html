BEST TIME TO VISIT VIGAN

Vigan enjoys two kinds of seasons, the dry season from
November to April and the wet season from May to October. It
is recommended that travelers visit during dry season, especially
from December to February when temperatures are much cooler
and touring will most likely not be interrupted by rains and will
not be really hot going around.
